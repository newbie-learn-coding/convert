FAQ

Will converting HEIC to JPG reduce quality?

At high quality settings, the difference is minimal. HEIC is more efficient, so JPG files will be larger.

Why can't I open HEIC files on my computer?

HEIC is Apple's format. Windows and some Android devices need additional software to support it.
