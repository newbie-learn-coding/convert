FAQ

What bitrate should I use for FLAC to MP3?

Use 320 kbps for best quality, 256 kbps for good quality with smaller files, or 192 kbps for spoken content.

Should I delete FLAC files after converting to MP3?

Keep FLAC files as masters if storage allows. You cannot recover FLAC quality from MP3 later.
