FAQ

Does PNG to JPG reduce file size?

Usually yes. JPG is lossy and optimized for photo-like images, so files are typically smaller than PNG exports.

Will my transparent PNG stay transparent?

No. JPG does not support transparency, so transparent areas become a solid background color.
