FAQ

Does converting MP3 to WAV improve quality?

No, the conversion cannot restore quality lost in the original MP3 compression. Use WAV for editing, not quality restoration.

Why do DAWs prefer WAV over MP3?

WAV is uncompressed, allowing precise editing without compression artifacts affecting the editing process.
