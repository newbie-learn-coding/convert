FAQ

Will I lose quality converting AVI to MP4?

With proper settings, quality loss is minimal. H.264 in MP4 often matches or exceeds older AVI codecs.

Why won't my AVI file play on Mac?

AVI uses various codecs, some proprietary to Windows. MP4 with H.264 works universally.
