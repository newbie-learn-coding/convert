FAQ

Will my fonts look the same in PDF?

If fonts are embedded, yes. Some systems substitute fonts if embedding fails or is disabled.

Can I edit a PDF after converting from DOCX?

PDFs are designed for fixed layouts. Editing requires specialized software and may not preserve formatting.
