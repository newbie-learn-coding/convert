FAQ

Why is my GIF file so large?

GIF is an older format that compresses less efficiently than MP4. Keep clips short and reduce colors to minimize size.

Can I convert long videos to GIF?

You can, but GIFs over a few seconds become very large. Consider keeping clips under 10 seconds.
